# Meridian Group — Multi-Entity Portfolio & Project Management Framework

This is a project-management and portfolio-governance methodology I designed and ran for a multi-entity professional-services holding company managing 50+ concurrent projects across subsidiaries (name changed for confidentiality). Read this as a framework, not a live company.

> **Disclaimer:** Company names, product names, and personal data in this repo are fictionalized to protect confidentiality. The methodology, structure, and outcomes are real.

---

## What's here

This repository is an [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/) vault that documents the full portfolio management system: meeting minutes, project logs, planning documents, SOPs, workflows, and weekly executive reports.

The vault was designed to be operated through Claude (Anthropic's AI assistant) using the Cowork desktop application — a natural-language interface that replaces what would otherwise be hours of manual administrative work. The methodology is real; the implementation toolchain is intentional.

### Contents

- **Organization/** — Team profiles, partner directory, and contact templates.
- **Knowledge-Base/** — 13 SOPs spanning the full project lifecycle (initiation through closure), 9 operational workflows (WF) for AI-assisted vault management, and 22 reusable templates (FOR).
- **Sample-Projects/** — Three complete anonymized projects showing the methodology in action: an Odoo CRM implementation, a file migration, and a payment confirmation agent.
- **Sample-Weekly-Reports/** — Two weekly executive portfolio summaries showing the reporting cadence and format.

### About the fictional company

The framework was built for a **financial operations services holding company** with multiple subsidiaries. The parent company provides technology-driven business process services; one subsidiary is a fintech building a payment and legal-tech platform; another handles financial restructuring operations. The names, products, and people have been replaced — the processes, templates, and artifacts are genuine.

---

## How the system works

The vault operates in two complementary modes:

**Reading and navigation** — from Obsidian. DataView dashboards and WikiLinks between notes give a navigable, up-to-date view of the portfolio.

**Writing and management** — from Claude via Cowork. A natural language instruction replaces manual admin work: generating a minute from a transcript, updating project logs, creating documents. This separation is intentional: Obsidian reads well, the AI writes well.
