# SOP-012 — Weekly Portfolio Executive Summary

## Purpose

Produce an executive summary of the portfolio status for a specific week, consolidating all vault activity into a single document presentable to the management team.

## When to use it

- Before the Monday Tactical Early Bird (preparation on Sunday or early Monday)
- When Management requests a portfolio status snapshot
- As input for presentations to external stakeholders

## How to invoke it with Cowork

> *"Prepare the executive summary for the week of [start date] to [end date]. [Optional: I want to highlight [project]. Attached is the upcoming week's calendar screenshot.]"*

Cowork executes the complete flow automatically.

## Output

| Artifact | Location | Name |
|---|---|---|
| Executive summary | `Reportes-Semanales/` | `Executive-Summary-Week-[NN]-[YYYY-MM-DD].md` |
