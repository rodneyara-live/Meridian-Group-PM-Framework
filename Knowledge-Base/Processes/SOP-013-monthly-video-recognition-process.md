# SOP-013 — Manager Recognition Monthly Video Process

> Monthly operational process for producing and publishing the recognition video for the manager team on T-Social. Originates from project [[PR-7-04_Internal-Communication-Campaign]], successfully closed on 2026-03-31 after validating the first iteration with positive team sentiment.

---

## Description

Recurring process for producing internal recognition content for the Meridian Group manager team. Uses Wolox weekly reports as data source, processes them with AI tools to generate scripts and voice, and assembles the final video in Canva for publication on T-Social.

**Frequency:** Monthly (publication in week 4 of each month)
**Main responsible:** [[Marisol-Guzman]]
**Review and publication:** [[Veronica-Bristow]]
**Data source:** [[Carlos-Ruiz]] (forwarding Wolox reports)

---

## Process Flow

### Step 1 — Weekly collection (each Monday of the month × 4 weeks)

| Who | What they do |
|---|---|
| Wolox (system) | Sends weekly performance summary of the manager team |
| [[Carlos-Ruiz]] | Receives the Wolox summary and forwards it to [[Marisol-Guzman]] |
| [[Marisol-Guzman]] | Transcribes the audio/summary with voice-to-text AI, generates the written summary and deposits it in the monthly Word template |

> ⚠️ Repeat all 4 Mondays of the month. By the end of week 4, [[Marisol-Guzman]] has 4 accumulated summaries.

---

### Step 2 — Monthly consolidation (week 4, before producing)

[[Marisol-Guzman]]:

1. Consolidates the 4 weekly summaries from the Word template
2. Extracts positive aspects and improvement opportunities for the month
3. Selects the **outstanding manager of each week** → 4 recognized managers in total
4. For each manager: generates the recognition audio script with AI

---

### Step 3 — Video production (week 4)

[[Marisol-Guzman]]:

1. Converts each audio script to voice following **Meridian Group brand standards**
2. Generates complementary images for the video
3. Assembles the 4 audios + images in **Canva** to produce the monthly video

---

### Step 4 — Review and publication (week 4)

[[Veronica-Bristow]]:

1. Reviews the assembled video
2. Publishes on **T-Social**

---
