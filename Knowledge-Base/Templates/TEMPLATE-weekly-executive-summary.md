> **Writing rules:**
> - Each paragraph has one central idea; do not mix projects of different weight in the same sentence.
> - Complete sentences, no stacked gerunds or lists disguised as prose.
> - Total length: 6–10 sentences distributed in 2–3 paragraphs. A single block of text is a formatting error.
> - Style: executive, direct, in good English. Without unnecessary jargon.
