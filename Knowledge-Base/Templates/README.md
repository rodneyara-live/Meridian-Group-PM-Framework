# Templates — Meridian Group Templates

This folder groups all reusable templates in the ecosystem: project forms and meeting minutes templates.

---

## Subfolders

### 📁 [[project-templates/README|project-templates/]]

The 14 standardized forms (FOR-001 to FOR-014) completed throughout a project's lifecycle — from the Project Sheet to the Final Report and Lessons Learned.

**When to use:** Whenever a [[../Processes/README|Processes/]] SOP indicates that a form needs to be completed.

---

### 🗒️ [[meeting-minutes/README|meeting-minutes/]]

The meeting minutes template (`MINUTES-template.md`) and the Claude prompt (`MINUTES-prompt.md`) to convert transcripts into actionable minutes integrated with the vault.

**When to use:** After each operational meeting, sprint, or any session that generates commitments or decisions.

---

---

### 📊 [[TEMPLATE-weekly-executive-summary|TEMPLATE-weekly-executive-summary.md]]

The canonical structure of the Weekly Portfolio Executive Summary that Cowork generates before each Tactical Early Bird. Contains all sections with their fill-in instructions.

**When to use:** Whenever the [[../Processes/SOP-012-weekly-executive-summary|SOP-012]] flow is executed.
