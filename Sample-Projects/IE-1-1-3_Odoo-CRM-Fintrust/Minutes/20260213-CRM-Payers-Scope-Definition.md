## 🏗️ Key Points of the Session

### Meridian Pay Starting Point

[[Tomas-Navarro]] explicitly clarified that **the Meridian Pay team does not know Odoo** — starting from scratch. At the management level, Bruno knows the project through the connection with senior management, but at the operational level no one at Meridian Pay has worked with the tool.

> "We can start from the fact that we know nothing."

### Sergio's Methodology for the Survey

[[Sergio-Mendoza]] explained his standard process:
1. **Week 1–2:** Videos / current flows → [[Sergio-Mendoza]] understands the context
2. **Week 2:** Graph and delimit the scope (the longest step)
3. **Week 3:** Figma mockups for visual validation
4. **Month 1:** Implementation post-mockup approval
5. **Total estimated:** 1 month (max month and a half)

[[Sergio-Mendoza]] emphasized his preference for grounding everything visually before coding, to avoid rework and align expectations.

> "Sometimes the analysis takes longer than the implementation — but that way we don't do double work."

### Bruno's Request: Written Scope

[[Tomas-Navarro]] requested that, once the scope is defined, a written document be drafted to review in a ~1 hour session with the team, in order to:
- Verify that no work is duplicated
- Confirm that the scope is neither too short nor too long
- Have clear expectations about deliverables

### Need to Align with Elena Torres

[[Rodney-Ramirez]] noted that the progress of this session needs to be **socialized with [[Elena-Torres]]**.
