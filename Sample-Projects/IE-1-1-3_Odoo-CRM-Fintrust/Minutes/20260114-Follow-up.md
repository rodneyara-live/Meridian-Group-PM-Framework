# IE-1-1-3 · 2026-01-14 · Weekly Follow-up: Model Validation with Hugo Pacheco + Carlos Ruiz

## Objective

Present the data model to Hugo Pacheco (judicial operations specialist) and Carlos Ruiz (business stakeholder) for operational validation.

## Session Highlights

Erick and Carlos Ruiz reviewed entity by entity. Main findings:

### Debtor Model

- ✅ Basic fields OK (name, ID number, address, phones, employer)
- ✅ Bank accounts list OK
- ✅ `debtor_type` (natural / legal entity) OK
- ❌ Missing field: **Sex** — Required for judicial statistics (how many men vs. women are being sued). The courts request this demographic data.
- ❌ Missing field: **Marital status** — Required for garnishment (affects the garnishable percentage). If married, the spouse may be a co-debtor.
- ❌ Missing field: **Birth date** — For locating the debtor (helps calculate age and identify whether the debtor is retired, etc.)
- ❌ Missing field: **Workplace address** — In addition to employer name. The exact workplace address is needed for court notifications.

### Case Model

- ✅ Case states for extrajudicial flow OK
- ✅ Debt block structure OK
- ❌ Missing: The concept of "updated debt" needs two values: **Original amount** vs. **Updated amount** (with interest + surcharges). Erick insists this is critical for negotiation.
- ❌ Missing: **Portfolio receipt date** — Different from case creation date. The portfolio is received on a specific date and the case can be created days later. Both dates must exist.

### Contact History

- ✅ Archiving changes (never delete) OK
- ✅ `is_active` flag OK

### Payment Agreements

- ✅ Structure OK

### Agreement Monitoring (New finding)

Erick mentioned that currently agreement monitoring is done in Excel. But a critical detail emerged: **agreement payments are received by the bank that originated the debt** (the creditor), not by Fintrust. Fintrust does not receive the money directly. This means:
- Fintrust does NOT have real-time payment confirmation.
- Odoo will need a field to **manually record** when the debtor pays and the creditor confirms → "Payment confirmed by creditor."
- The payments are not automatic; they are at the creditor's pace.

## Parking Lot

- **Credit bureau integration:** Carlos Ruiz asked if Odoo can query debtor history with credit bureaus. Not for MVP. Goes to parking lot.
- **Automated notifications to debtors:** Email or SMS sending. Not MVP. Goes to parking lot.
- **Courier / notification management** (court notifications): Not MVP. Goes to parking lot.
