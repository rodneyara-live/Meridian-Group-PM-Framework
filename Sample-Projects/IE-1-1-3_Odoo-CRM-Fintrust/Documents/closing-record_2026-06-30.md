## 5. Lessons Learned

- **Milestone-based pricing > hourly pricing** — the hourly model generated a load that was difficult to control during the accounting project; agreed that future Odoo projects with [[Sergio-Mendoza]] / [[Gustavo-Novak]] will be milestone-based. Decision validated by [[Elena-Torres]] and [[Rodney-Ramirez]].
- **UAT with the end user ([[Hugo-Pacheco]]) is the most critical step** — observations not confirmed as critical vs. cosmetic caused delays; define UAT finding classification from kickoff.
- **Separating CRM from the transactional module from the start** prevented scope creep; Milestone 3 remained as Fintrust's decision, not as a project pending item.
