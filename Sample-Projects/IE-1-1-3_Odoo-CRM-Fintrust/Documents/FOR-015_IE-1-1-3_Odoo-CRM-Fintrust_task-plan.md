## Plan Notes

- **Critical assumption:** Formal development start (Phase 1) depends on delivery of access credentials to Odoo v17 environments. Committed date: week of 2026-02-17. Any delay shifts all following phases in direct proportion.
- **Fixed architectural decision:** Case status in Odoo is NEVER manually modified — only Bonita updates states. BonitaOdoo integration (task 2.1) is NON-NEGOTIABLE from MVP.
- **Contact history:** Phones, emails and addresses are NEVER deleted — only deactivated. This must be reflected in task 1.2 development.
- **Scope control:** Additional changes to the agreed scope go to parking lot and are billed separately. The PM is responsible for applying this control.
- **Overall progress — manual calculation:** Sum the % of all 20 tasks and divide by 20. Example at Phase 1 close: 10 tasks at 100% + 4 at 100% = 1400/20 = 70%.
- **Andira Partners:** Odoo Partner receiving the technical handoff at closure. Must be informed of progress from Milestone 1.
