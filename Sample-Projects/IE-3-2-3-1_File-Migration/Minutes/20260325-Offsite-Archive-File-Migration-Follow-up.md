### Block 3 — Floor 6 Space (new file area location)

Gregory raised an operational concern: the space assigned to the file area on floor 6 is insufficient for ongoing operations.

**The problem:**
- The file area needs space to **receive and process batches of 10 to 20 containers** each time attorneys deliver legal documentation (this happens continuously even without maintaining a massive physical file).
- Gregory reviewed the floor 6 space with Carlos Ruiz and indicated there is no room to open a work table or to receive those boxes.

**Conversation with Luis:**
Gregory suggested to Carlos Ruiz the possibility of enabling an additional area behind floor 6. Carlos Ruiz indicated it would need to be consulted with "the doctor" (who wants to keep that room for his use). Rodney noted that space is a real limitation for everyone in this transition period.

**[[Rodney-Ramirez]]'s stance:**
- Space is one of the project's big challenges, along with the offsite archive migration.
- The goal is to make the process as fluid, efficient and controlled as possible — documents should enter and exit quickly to minimize the physical space needed at any given moment.
- Space is complicated for the entire organization. Nothing can be guaranteed today. They will work with what is available and adapt as they go.
- He asked Gregory to **convey that same positive attitude to the staff**: it's a change that is uncomfortable at first but improves with time.

**Immediate action agreed:**
[[Elena-Torres]] reported that **that same day (03/25) they will begin moving available desks to floor 6** — selecting the smallest and best-condition ones. This way, when it's time to move people with their equipment, they already have a place for their computer and chair.
