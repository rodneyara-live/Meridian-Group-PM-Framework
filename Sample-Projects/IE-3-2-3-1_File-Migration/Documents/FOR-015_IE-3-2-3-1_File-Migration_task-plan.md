## Plan Notes

- **Estimated dates:** Dates for phases 1–5 are estimates based on charter durations. PM must confirm and adjust during formal kickoff with sponsors.
- **Critical pilot phase (3.1):** The pilot test with a non-critical batch is mandatory before moving regulatory or high-criticality documents. This stage cannot be compressed.
- **Personnel transition (3.5):** Must be closely coordinated with HR from Phase 2. Timing of communication to affected personnel is strategic.
- **Overall progress — calculation:** Sum of % for all 24 tasks divided by 24. Currently: 4 tasks at 100% + 1 task at 50% + rest at 0% = 450/24 ≈ 19% (adjusted to 15% by PM, weighting quality of progress vs. mechanical %).
- **Scope control:** Any scope change goes to parking lot and is managed via FOR-009.
- **Critical constraint:** Access to critical documents cannot be compromised at any time. High-turnover documents are digitized or kept in-house until the provider demonstrates stable SLA compliance (Phase 4).
